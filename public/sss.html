📋 General Questions

🎯 What is NICHIFY PRO? +
NICHIFY PRO is an AI-powered analysis platform that helps you find profitable YouTube niches. With 75+ detailed niches, live YouTube data, AI assistant, channel analysis, risk detector and more, we help content creators make the right decisions — in minutes, not weeks.
🆓 Is there a free version? +
Yes! You can sign up for free and try basic features with limited usage. When you purchase Lifetime PRO, you get unlimited access to all features forever.
🌍 Do you support other languages? +
The interface is currently available in English and Turkish. However, our analysis engine works in all languages. So you can research YouTube channels in English, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Arabic, Turkish and more from anywhere in the world. More interface languages coming soon based on community votes!
📱 Can I use it on mobile? +
Yes! NICHIFY PRO is fully mobile responsive. You can use it seamlessly on any browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox) on your phone or tablet. Native iOS and Android apps are coming in the upcoming months!
🗳️ Can I suggest new features? +
Absolutely! We have a public roadmap where you can suggest features and vote on what we build next. Your voice shapes the future of NICHIFY!

Yes! All data is transmitted over SSL-encrypted connections. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text). Our Supabase infrastructure is GDPR compliant.

NICHIFY PRO works seamlessly on all modern browsers:
• Chrome (recommended)
• Firefox
• Safari
• Edge
• Opera
• Mobile Safari (iOS)
• Chrome Mobile (Android)
💻 Can I use it offline? +
No, NICHIFY PRO requires an internet connection because we pull live data from YouTube. When our mobile app launches, an offline mode may be added.

Lifetime PRO members get unlimited usage! Free users are limited to 10 searches per day, 3 channel analyses, and 5 AI messages.
