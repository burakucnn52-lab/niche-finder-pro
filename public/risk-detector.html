NICHIFY Risk Detector analyzes your channel based on 10+ criteria and measures its compliance with YouTube's community guidelines.

✅ High score (80-100): Safe, low termination risk
⚠️ Medium score (50-79): Some improvements needed

✅ DO's

  • 🎨 Original content: Film with your own camera, create your own graphics
  • 🎵 Royalty-free music: Use YouTube Audio Library, Epidemic Sound
  • 📝 Clear descriptions: Add sources to your video descriptions
  • ⏰ Consistent uploads: 1-3 videos per week, on a regular schedule
  • 👥 Engagement: Reply to comments, build a community
  • 📊 Track analytics: Monitor your data from YouTube Studio
  • 🎯 Niche focus: Specialize in a single topic

❌ DON'Ts

  • 🚫 Reupload: Don't re-upload someone else's videos
  • 🎵 Copyrighted music: Don't use popular songs without permission
  • 📺 Movie/TV clips: Unauthorized use of movie scenes
  • 🤖 Fully AI: Only AI voice + AI visuals = ban risk
  • 🔞 Kids content (COPPA): Don't miscategorize your content
  • 💊 Misleading health claims: Don't give medical advice
  • 👁️ Clickbait: Overly exaggerated titles/thumbnails
