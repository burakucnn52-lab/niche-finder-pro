📊 1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up for NICHIFY PRO, we collect the following information:

  • Email address: For account creation and communication
  • Name: For your profile (optional)
  • Password: Stored encrypted with Bcrypt
  • Profile picture: If you sign in with Google

Usage Data

  • Search history (niche searches)
  • Favorite niches
  • Page view statistics
  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address (for general location detection)

Payment Information

Payment transactions are processed through LemonSqueezy. Your credit card information is never stored on our servers. We only receive the transaction ID and amount information.

🎯 2. How We Use Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • ✅ To provide and improve our services
  • ✅ To manage your account and ensure your security
  • ✅ To offer you personalized recommendations
  • ✅ To send emails about important updates
  • ✅ To provide customer support
  • ✅ To fulfill legal obligations
  • ✅ To create anonymous statistics and analytics

🤝 3. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. Limited sharing only occurs in the following cases:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), LemonSqueezy (payment), Google (analytics)
  • Legal obligation: In case of a court order or legal request
  • Company merger: You will be notified if this happens in the future

🛡️ 4. Data Security

Measures we take to ensure the security of your data:

  • 🔐 SSL/TLS encryption on all connections
  • 🔐 Bcrypt for password hashing
  • 🔐 Row Level Security (RLS) for database protection
  • 🔐 OAuth 2.0 for Google login
  • 🔐 Regular security updates
  • 🔐 GDPR-compliant infrastructure

🍪 5. Cookie Policy

NICHIFY PRO uses the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies: For site functionality (session, language preference)
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Preference cookies: For personalization (theme, favorites)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some features.

⚖️ 6. Your User Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • ✓ The right to know whether your personal data is being processed
  • ✓ The right to request information about your processed data
  • ✓ The right to request correction of your data
  • ✓ The right to request deletion of your data
  • ✓ The right to request notification to third parties
  • ✓ The right to object to automated analysis results
  • ✓ The right to claim compensation for damages

To exercise these rights, you can email bucun648@gmail.com.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

NICHIFY PRO does not serve users under the age of 18. Accounts we detect as belonging to anyone under 18 are deleted immediately. If you believe your child has shared their data, please contact us.

📝 8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes:

  • 📧 We will notify you by email
  • 🔔 We will display a notification on the site
  • 📅 We will indicate the update date at the top
